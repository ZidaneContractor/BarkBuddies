Meet the Owners of Bark Buddies

Zidane

Zidane has always had a deep love for animals, especially dogs. Growing up with a furry companion instilled in them the importance of responsible and loving pet care. With a background in animal behavior studies, Zidane brings a wealth of knowledge to Bark Buddies, ensuring every dog receives personalized attention and a safe environment. Their vision is to create a community where dog owners can have complete peace of mind while they're away.

Yuvaraj

Yuvaraj's passion for entrepreneurship combined with a genuine care for dogs led to the inception of Bark Buddies. With a strong business acumen and a knack for organization, Yuvaraj manages the operational aspects of the company, ensuring smooth and efficient service delivery. They believe in building strong relationships with both the dogs and their owners, fostering trust and reliability.

Yashas

Yashas's gentle and patient nature makes them a natural with dogs of all temperaments and sizes. Their experience in handling various breeds and understanding their unique needs is invaluable to Bark Buddies. Yashas is committed to providing a fun, engaging, and comfortable experience for every dog in their care. They are also a certified pet first-aider, ensuring the safety and well-being of all furry clients.

Yogesh

Yogesh brings a creative and energetic approach to dog sitting. They are passionate about providing stimulating activities and ensuring dogs get plenty of exercise and playtime. With a background in canine enrichment, Yogesh designs tailored routines to keep dogs happy and entertained. Their dedication to creating a positive and joyful atmosphere is a cornerstone of the Bark Buddies philosophy.
